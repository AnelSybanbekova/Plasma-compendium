Welcome

Plasma Compendium is an educational resource dedicated to plasma physics. The goal of this website is to provide concise explanations of key concepts, from atomic physics foundations to modern fusion research challenges.

My name is Anel Sybanbekova, and I created this website as an educational project for everyone interested in plasma physics.

The purpose of this compendium is to make plasma physics more accessible for beginners, explaining fundamental concepts step by step, from the basics of atomic physics and plasma properties to modern research topics such as fusion energy and plasma confinement.

This project combines my interest in physics, scientific communication, and the desire to create a structured resource where anyone can start learning about plasma from the very beginning.
