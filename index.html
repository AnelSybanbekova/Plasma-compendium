Welcome

Plasma Compendium is an educational resource dedicated to plasma physics. The goal of this website is to provide concise explanations of key concepts, from atomic physics foundations to modern fusion research challenges.

Plasma Diagnostics

Diagnostics are techniques used to measure plasma properties, including Langmuir probes, spectroscopy, interferometry, and Thomson scattering.

Current Research Challenges

Modern plasma research focuses on fusion energy, turbulence, plasma-wall interactions, advanced diagnostics, and high-performance simulations.
